About SRH

The Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) is a franchise cricket team based in Hyderabad, Telangana. Founded in 2012, SRH has consistently been one of the most competitive teams in the IPL.

History

SRH replaced the Deccan Chargers in the IPL. Known for their strong bowling lineup and consistent performances, SRH quickly became a formidable team in the league.

Championships

  • 2016: SRH won their first IPL title under the captaincy of David Warner, defeating Royal Challengers Bangalore in the final.

Performance Overview

Sunrisers Hyderabad has been a consistent performer in the IPL, known for their strong bowling attack and competitive spirit.

  • Consistent Playoff Appearances: SRH has consistently qualified for the playoffs in multiple IPL seasons.
  • Championship Title: SRH won the IPL title in 2016, defeating Royal Challengers Bangalore in the final.
  • Runners-up: They reached the IPL final again in 2018, showcasing their competitiveness.
  • Bowling Prowess: Known for their formidable bowling lineup with players excelling in taking wickets and maintaining low economy rates.

Records and Notable Achievements

  • Most Wickets: Bhuvneshwar Kumar holds the record for the most wickets for SRH.
  • Highest Run-scorer: David Warner has amassed the highest runs for SRH.
  • Best Bowling Figures: Rashid Khan holds the record for the best bowling figures in an IPL match for SRH.
  • Team Records: SRH holds various team records in IPL, including the highest team total and lowest total defended.
  • Captaincy Milestones: Captains like David Warner and Kane Williamson have led SRH with distinction.
